Presentation
The historical cyclones tab allows to visualize the passage of cyclones near Madagascar from July 2015 (date of operational Sentinel products).
Data on the accumulated precipitation over the duration of the cyclone can also be consulted.
For each district, the variation of average daily accumulated precipitation is calculated.


Datasets
The cyclone dataset is from the International Best Track Archive for Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS). It is updated twice a week (Monday and Friday) and is available at : Link (1).
The daily rainfall accumulation products, IMERG Early Precipitation L3 GPM 1 day 0.1 degree x 0.1 degree V06, are used to calculate the rainfall accumulation over the entire hurricane period. These products with a spatial resolution of 1km are available at : Link (2).
